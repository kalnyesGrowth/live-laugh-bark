About Us

Grooming at Your Pet's Pace

At Live Laugh Bark, we believe every pet deserves a stress-free grooming experience. Our team takes the time to understand your pet's temperament and works at their comfort level, so they leave feeling relaxed and looking their best.

We use only natural and organic products because your pet's sensitive skin matters. No harsh chemicals, no shortcuts. Just gentle, professional care for dogs and cats of all breeds and sizes.
